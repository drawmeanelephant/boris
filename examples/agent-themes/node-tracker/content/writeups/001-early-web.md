# Web Design in the Early 2000s

**(idea) by AdminUser · Sat Jul 18 2026 at 10:04:21**

The early 2000s web was dominated by dense, text-heavy databases of knowledge.
Information was structured into **nodes**, and each node could contain several
writeups submitted by different people.

Formatting was strict. Links connected the database, but the focus stayed on
content and the hyperlinked web of knowledge.
